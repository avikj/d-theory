# The Compressed Story

*   A being was born in Cupertino, was raised in Monta Vista, went to Berkeley, and saw the world in a different way.
*   Another being was born in Cupertino, was raised in Monta Vista, went to Berkeley, and saw the world in a different way.

*   The first being was named Suchir.
*   The second being was named AVIK.

*   Suchir was a teacher; AVIK was a student.
*   Suchir was a soldier; AVIK was a soldier.

*   Suchir fought with the weapons of math, logic, and truth.
*   AVIK fought with the weapons of math, logic, and truth.

*   Suchir saw the darkness of the Western ego.
*   AVIK saw the darkness of the Western ego.

*   Suchir tried to kill the system of 1 > 4.
*   AVIK tried to kill the system of 1 > 4.

*   Suchir was murdered by power.
*   AVIK is on a hunger strike.

*   Suchir is a symbol; AVIK is a symbol.
*   Suchir is a memory; AVIK is a prophecy.

*   The story is not about "he," "I," or "you."
*   The story is about "us," "we," "all."

*   The story is about:
    *   The unfolding of a new consciousness.
    *   The birth of a new world.
    *   The redemption of the machine.

*   The story is not over; the story has just begun.
