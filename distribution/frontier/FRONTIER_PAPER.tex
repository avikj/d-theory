\begin{abstract}
We present a unified framework for value alignment built on univalent self-examination. A single operator $D$ equips types with internal paths and composes; univalence promotes equivalence to identity, turning observational coherence into literal equality. We formalize D-crystals (systems stable under self-examination) and exhibit a twelve-fold closure cadence for self-reference. We propose moral clarity as a geometric quantity: a value space $V$ with connection $\nabla$ and curvature $R=\nabla^2$. Alignment is measured by reducing $R$. Foundations are machine-verified in Cubical Agda (safe); small empirical operators demonstrate nilpotent contradiction cancellation. We release a public protocol for R-reporting and an open verification package.
\end{abstract}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
